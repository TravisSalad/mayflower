About Us

Mayflower Laundry & Drycleaning Co. was founded in Hartford in 1929 and is still operated by the founding family.
Ask us about that story and receive $5 off your next incoming Drycleaning order(no other discounts apply).
We care about our customers, and do our very best for everyone who patrons our store at 266 Prospect Avenue, Hartford, CT.

"You look great in everything we do!"

For all your laundry and drycleaning services:
Drycleaning - Shirts done the way you like them; box or hanger, starch or no starch
Laundry - sheets crisply pressed
Table Linens - expertly pressed
Area Rugs
Leather/Suede/UGGS
Wedding Gowns/Christening Gowns – treasure chest preservation service
